HOW BUSINESS FUNDING BY ALPHA FINANCE GROUP
WORKS

-NO FIXED PAYMENTS – PAY AS YOU GO!

There are never any fixed payments with us, we collect an agreed-upon percentage of your sales, so if your sales drop so do the payments. We can reconcile payments based on sales daily, weekly, or monthly.

-WHO CAN I CONTACT WITH ANY QUESTIONS?

Call us! anytime, Our funding specialists are waiting to help you.

-IS MY INFORMATION SECURE?

Yes, we take protecting your information seriously. Please see our Privacy Policy.

-WHAT DOCUMENTS ARE REQUIRED FOR BUSINESS FUNDING?

To get started, Apply Here. Then we will request a few documents to demonstrate the performance of your business and your last 4 months of Business Bank Statements. That’s it.

-HOW WILL -MY CREDIT IMPACT MY APPLICATION?

Here at Alpha Financial Groups we don’t let Credit tell the whole story. We want to understand you credit history and learn about your business as a whole.

-HOW SOON WILL I HAVE ACCESS TO FUNDS?

Once you’ve completed the funding process, you can have access to funds within 24 hours, sometimes even sooner!

-HOW IS BUSINESS FUNDING REPAYMENT MADE?

We offer repayments via ACH from your Business Bank Account or via Credit Cards. You deliver the specified percentage of receivables on a daily basis.

-ARE YOU A DIRECT FUNDER?

Yes! We are a Direct Funding source.

-WHAT IF I DON’T QUALIFY FOR FUNDING?

You’ll never know until you fill in your profile. If you are one of the few we can’t help then we’ll tell you straight away and let you know how you can improve.

-WHAT CAN I USE THE FUNDS FOR?

Funding must be used for business purposes, and you are in control of determining how to best use the capital for your business. Some examples are: Payroll, Remodeling,Inventory, Working Capital, Expansion Marketing Equipment
