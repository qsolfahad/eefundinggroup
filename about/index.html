About Us

BUSINESS FUNDING BY E&E FUNDING GROUP

OUR MISSION IS TO CREATE OPPORTUNITIES FOR BUSINESSES AND CHAMPION ENTREPRENEURS WHO ARE THE LIFEBLOOD OF OUR ECONOMY.

Since 2015, E&E FUNDING GROUP has become a leader in business funding. We have worked with over 90,000 merchants in a variety of industries like auto repair, rehabilitation centers, landscaping businesses, construction contractors, and more. We understand the challenges that businesses face and are passionate about providing alternative funding to help them succeed. E&E FUNDING GROUP takes pride in our straightforward and transparent approach that considers your business’s big picture, not just your credit score. This commitment to creating access to capital for our clients has enabled us to offer over $900 million in funding options for our small businesses across the nation.

All too often, entrepreneurs don’t have the funds they need on hand to reach the next phase of their company’s evolution. And the amount of red tape and waiting around they have to endure to secure financing is often not worth the hassle. That’s where Freedom Financial Group’s years of experience providing business owners with prompt and personal funding makes a big difference. Whether you’re looking to purchase inventory, expand your location, hire new staff, or grow your revenue, E&E FUNDING GROUP is there to make sure you have the capital you need to realize your dreams. We’re more than just another business funder. We’re a funding partner that is with you for the long haul.

HELPING BUSINESSES GROW

Do you feel like you’re on the cusp of something big but don’t have the capital to get there? E&E FUNDING GROUP has worked with businesses across the country that faced the same issue. Whether you’re interested in renovating your location, implementing a fresh marketing campaign, buying inventory at a discount, or covering payroll interruptions, E&$ Funding Group is familiar with your circumstances and wants to be part of your solution. When it comes to helping small businesses reach their full potential, Freedom Financial is passionate about being part of that journey.

A NEW APPROACH TO FUNDING

Our approach to funding is rooted in our commitment to the success of businesses like yours. We understand the unique stressors that seeking financing can cause, and we endeavor to eliminate as much anxiety and obscurity from our funding process as possible. Our application is straightforward, approvals are often handled in hours, and you can have the financing in your account within the same week that you’ve applied, and sometimes as soon as 24 hours. But, best of all, we consider the overall financial health and trajectory of your business rather than a glance at your credit score. If you have questions, our in-house underwriters will walk your through every aspect of your funding package to be certain that it is working for you.
